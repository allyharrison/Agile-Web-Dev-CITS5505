Hearth Restaurant & Lounge

1 Barrack St, Perth Western Australia 6000

Hearth is the heart of an unparalleled Western Australian dining experience located at The Ritz-Carlton. Hearth features seasonal menus using the freshest ingredients brought to life in our custom hearth. Set against a relaxed and elegant atmosphere it is infused with warm Australian culture.

Mechanics Institute Bar

REAR/222 William St, Northbridge WA 6000

Nestled amongst the heritage rooftops of Northbridge, Mechanics' Institute offers an escape from the every day below. With al fresco roof deck seating, sing-out-loud sound tracks and epic cocktails, Mechanics' is a small bar that’s big on fun.

Bobeche

Basement, 131 St Georges Terrace, Perth WA 6000

Celebrate the art of a well-crafted cocktail at Bobeche, one of Perth’s most charismatic cocktail bars. Descend to The Heritage basement and find a century old, speak-easy vibe with some of the best bartenders in town.

Sneaky Tony’s

Somewhere in Chinatown, Northbridge, WA 6003

A homage to infamous rum-runner Tony “the hat”, Sneaky Tony’s is a prohibition bar with shelves bursting with liquid gold. With over 300 bottles of rum from all 4 corners of the globe, pull up a stool and let us pour you a dram.

Alabama Song

232 William St, Perth WA 6000

Alabama Song is a little bit honky-tonk and a whole lotta country. Specialising in whiskey and bourbon, you’re guaranteed to have a rockin’ good time once you enter through the bat swing doors at Alabama.
